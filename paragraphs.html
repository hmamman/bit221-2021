Memos

A memo is any paper document that is used for official communication between two parties. The communication can be Organisation-to-Organisation or Department-to-Department. External-In memo is a memo coming into the organisation from another organisation while a memo that goes out to another organisation is called Internal-Out memo. Memos that are initiated and terminated within the organisation are Internal memos. Memos are kept and moved from office-to-office within the organisation in a sheet of half-folded heavy paper material called file folder.

File folder, or simply file, can be classified as either open file or secret file. Open files are used for keeping internal memos and also moving them around from office-to-office within the organisation and are found in open registry and other departments and units within the organisation. Departments and units can have as many open files as they require.
